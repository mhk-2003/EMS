Welcome to our Entertainment Management System!

Entertainment management is a relatively new business management discipline that is
increasingly being taught as a Bachelor of Science Degree. Entertainment management
courses aim to provide graduates with appropriate knowledge and skills to progress into
management careers within the entertainment, managing aspiring and established artists
and entertainers, as well as facilities such as sports events, theme parks, theaters, cinemas,
live music venues, museums, art galleries, broadcast media companies and night clubs.
